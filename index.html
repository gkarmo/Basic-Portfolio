About Me

Gayah Karmo born 1996 and still doing my thing. Born in Monrovia, Liberia and migrated here with my family December of 1999, and even though I did not have much say in that decision, it has definately the most impactful one in my life. This supporting cast starts with my parents, whom since I can remember is always pulling a double shifts multiple times a week or coming home to grab a bite before heading off to a second job. It wasn't only the hard work they exhitbited and instilled in my brothers and I, but also the displine, confindence, self-love, and respect and love for others that developed my second tier of support, my brothers.g my thing.
